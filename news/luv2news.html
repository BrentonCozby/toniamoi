LUV IS RAGE 2 IS HERE


Lil Uzi Vert’s highly anticipated album has been here for about a week now and already the tracks have racked up millions of views on his YouTube channel. I will be reviewing the whole album soon, but after listening to it a few times, a couple tracks, “The Way Life Goes” and “Neon Guts,” featuring Pharrell, really stood out to me. The production on both tracks are basically seamless. One being an instrumental of an indie pop song called, “Landslide” by London pop-duo, Oh Wonder, and the other, a Pharrell Williams-produced four-count intro track that sounds as colorful, bouncy, and bright as Lil Uzi Vert’s care-free attitude and the clothes he parades around with in public.
